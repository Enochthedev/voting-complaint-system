# Analytics Tab - Quick Reference Guide

## Overview
The Analytics tab in the lecturer dashboard provides comprehensive insights into complaint management performance, trends, and satisfaction metrics.

### 2. Complaints Over Time
- **Type**: Bar chart
- **Data**: 13 days of complaint submissions

- **Insights**: Peak day, daily average, period change

### 3. Complaints by Status
- **Type**: Progress bars
- **Categories**: New, Opened, In Progress, Resolved, Closed
- **Features**: Color-coded, percentage display
- **Insights**: Open vs completed cases

### 4. Complaints by Category
- **Type**: Bar chart
- **Categories**: 6 complaint categories
- **Features**: Hover tooltips, color-coded
- **Insights**: Top category, distribution

### 5. Complaints by Priority
- **Type**: Bar chart
- **Levels**: Critical, High, Medium, Low
- **Features**: Severity-based colors
- **Insights**: Priority distribution

### 6. Lecturer Performance
- **Type**: Data table
- **Columns**: Name, Handled, Avg Response, Resolution Rate, Rating
- **Features**: Sortable, color-coded badges

- **Type**: Detailed breakdown
- **Features**: Overall rating, star display, distribution
- **Insights**: Rating trends, distribution by stars

## Color Coding

### Status Colors
- **Blue**: New complaints
- **Yellow**: Opened/In Progress
- **Purple**: In Progress
- **Green**: Resolved/Completed
- **Gray**: Closed

### Priority Colors
- **Red**: Critical
- **Orange**: High
- **Yellow**: Medium
- **Green**: Low

### Performance Colors
- **Green**: Excellent (90%+)
- **Blue**: Good (80-89%)
- **Red**: Needs Improvement (<80%)
